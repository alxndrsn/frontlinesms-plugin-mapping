The View menu

In the Classic and New View, the View menu allows you to configure what you see on your screen


Switch to Classic View / New View

Depending on which view you are currently in, the "Switch..." entry allows you to switch back and forth to the other. In FrontlineSMS, "Classic View" maintains the original look and feel of the earlier version of the software. "New View" carries additional and advanced functionality, and carries improved sending and receiving speed. You can switch views any time you like, as many times as you like


Tabs ("New View" only)

In "New View", the Tabs menu allows you to select which tabs you see along the top of your screen. If, for example, you don't want to see the Forms tab (if you don't intend using this function), you can hide it by removing the tick on the "Forms" entry in the menu. Tabs can be displayed or hidden as you see fit, and as many times as you like, whenever you like
